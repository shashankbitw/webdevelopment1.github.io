Walkathon SelectHER Adoption of Shettigere Village SMART Centre Innaugration CSR for Whirlpool

As part of community outreach program UIIC, REVA University joined hands with Motherhood India for the Nobel task â€œ Beti Bachao Beti Padhao walkathon" on Daughter's day (24 Sep 2017). The registration fees of this event will go to Swanthana charitable trust which houses 50 girls between the ages 8 months and 15 years, who are mentally challenged and have multiple disabilities.

Select HER is a demand-driven corporate initiative to skill and empower underprivileged women for careers in lifestyle and fashion retail in India.It is a carefully designed, mentor-led, advanced skill training program which incorporates retail skills, life skills as well as on-job-training.

As per the initiative and guidelines of Government of Karnataka, REVA University adopted Shettigere Village, Jala hobli near International airport, to develop it as a SMART Village. As a part of inaugural programme, World Environment day was also celebrated by tree plantation by our Hon'ble Chancellor Dr. P. Shamaraju Sir along with Vice chancellor Dr S Y Kulkarni and Dr M Dhanamjaya Registrar.

REVA University joined hands with Tech Mahindra Foundation CSR Initiative to empower youth of the country by Inaugurating SMART Centre at REVA University Campus.

REVA UIIC, in association with Team Lease executed the CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) project of Whirlpool in the month of June 2017. For the first batch, there were 25 students who got the benefit out of this project. Certified students entered into OJT (on job training) with Rs. 7250/- as Stipend.
